Tickets for Japan Habba 2023 sold out with a week to go for the event, so we took a year to figure out where to head next. It's time for us to double the capacity for the event, by quadrupling the capacity. That's quadruple the fun, with more booths, activities, events, performances, and eyeballs than ever before. We're ready to power up India's biggest celebration of Japanese culture, and take it to the next level.


We've got our fingers on the pulse of the fandom.

Japan Habba has grown alongside and learned from the growing anime community in India. We run India's largest anime quiz since 2018. Our committee members have their fingers on the pulse of the anime fandom, and can help showcase your brand to a vibrant community of anime fans. We strictly do not allow counterfeit merchandise at our event.


The power of community. We're a non-profit.

The organizers of Japan Habba are active representatives from the Bangalore Japanese language and culture communities, or reputed Japanese organizations - with a common goal of promoting Japanese culture in Bangalore, and encouraging mutual cultural exchange between India and Japan.
